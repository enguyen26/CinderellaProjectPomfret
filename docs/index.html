The Cinderella Project

A social-impact web app designed for confidence, belonging, and access: students can browse donated dresses, filter by real needs, and use AI styling support for milestone events.

What I Want You To Notice

I focused on real-world usefulness, not just visuals: mission-first design, fast browsing, and thoughtful AI support that feels warm and inclusive.
